# SQA-Portfolio
My portfolio of real-world SQA testing work performed on company projects/ products, featuring their URLs and screenshots as evidence of testing across multiple platforms.

## About Me
SQA Engineer with 3.5 years of professional experience in Manual Testing, Automation Testing, API Testing, Performance Testing and Backend Validation. Skilled in CI/CD pipeline testing, SQL database validation, and manual/automation testing for web, mobile, and desktop applications. Experienced in Selenium, Playwright, Postman, and JMeter, with strong exposure to Agile environments and DevOps collaboration. Proven ability to ensure end-to-end QA lifecycle ownership, product quality, and system stability through structured testing and automation practices. Demonstrated Leadership Skills by mentoring interns and managing responsibilities independently. Recognized as an All-Rounder SQA Professional with practical exposure beyond traditional testing including Automation, Debugging, Backend Systems, DevOps Collaboration, AI Tools, Data Validation, and Process Optimization, rather than being limited to only writing test cases and reporting bugs.
